Driver Application Form

Thank you for being interested in employment opportunities with Russo Brothers Transportation Inc. This application form consists of 16 steps and normally takes up to an hour to complete. We are actively seeking for experienced CDL Class-A drivers to join our team. We are looking for both company drivers and owner-operators. We primarily work with temperature controlled trailers providing flexible and consistent working hours.

Qualifications

  • CDL Class-A license
  • Minimum 23 years of age
  • Meet DOT medical requirements
  • At least 2 years of verifiable experience driving commercial vehicles

Requirements

Before starting, please make sure that you have scanned copies of following documents on your device. You will be asked to upload these documents on the first step of the application:
  • Copy of a Driver's License
  • Copy of a Medical Card
  • Copy of Social Security Card
  • DMV Printout for the last 3 Years
